Welcome to the GEOG 456 Final Project website. This project explores the history of lesbian nightlife spaces in the United States through an interactive geovisualization and narrative storytelling.

Project Significance

This project which studies and celebrates lesbian spaces is very important to us as queer people and scholars. Lesbian bars and clubs have been historically underinvested, understudied, and underappreciated, particularly in comparison to gay spaces. Because of the important role these spaces have played for many people, we sought to learn more about their history and visualize their changes over time.
